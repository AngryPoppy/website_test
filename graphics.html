NCC Logo

Made April 21, 2020

Above is a new logo I made for Nashua Community College for use in a variety of documentation. This logo was the final result after many drafts and edits. It was requested by Barry Meehan of Nashua Community College and was approved by him. This logo is a perfect example of how even the simplest design can be striking and effective, while showcasing the core colors associated with Nashua Community College; White, Teal, and Blue.
